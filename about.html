Our Journey

Founded in the heart of Adamson University in 2023, ADT - Ace's Demon Tech began as a small startup with a big dream: to revolutionize the gaming industry. Our founders, a group of passionate gamers and tech enthusiasts, noticed a gap in the market for high-performance gaming technology that didn't compromise on innovation or design. From our humble beginnings in a cramped garage, we've grown into a leading name in gaming technology, known for our cutting-edge products that push the boundaries of what's possible.
